Site Help

Creating an Account

An account is a virtual representation of money organization. Accounts may exist in the real world, they may be virtual accounts (such as money allocated or equity), or they may be invoice accounts (accounts payable and recievable).

You are free to use accounts however you like to structure things in whatever manner seems appropriate to your organization. To manage accounts, go to the accounts tab.

Creating a Transaction

To move money between (or into) accounts, you need a transaction. Transactions are entites representing the transfer of cash from one account to another, and the only way to update account balances. In proper double-entry booking style, any transaction must have both an account it comes out of and an account it goes into. It is appropriate to maintain "external" accounts like income, expenditure, and equity accounts to represent money leaving and entering the organization.

To manage transactions, go to the transactions tab.
